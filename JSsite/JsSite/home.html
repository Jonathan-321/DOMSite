A Memoir...

One of the most prevalent economic activities in my hometown is fishing. Normally, fishermen go out at Lake Kivu in the morning and return in the evening with their catch. The fish are then sold in the local market or exported to other parts of the country. I have been fishing with my friends and sometimes with siblings since I was a child, We used some thin bamboo trees grown around the lake,some fishing nets and finally used steples as needles to pinch and catch fish. Although I rarely make time for it now, it is one of my favorite pastimes.

This is also a beautiful panoramic view of the calm, dark waters of Lake Kivu in Gisenyi. Palm trees sway in the foreground with green hills rising in the background. It's a relaxing scene.
